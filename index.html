Story

Our Restaurant

Akai was born out of a vision to provide a simple, yet warm and comfortable dining experience. It all started with an idea of three friends, gaming and food lovers, to bring together each of their own strenghts, to create something they can share with others. With Chef Ajay – a natural artist, with an immense passion in culinary art, Ketan – a Computer Engineer with business acumen, and Jitender, a lawyer with exceptional culinary knowledge, Akai Restaurant was born.
Akai is your place to enjoy good food with the comfort and familiarity of home! We only strive to give you the warmest of welcome and service you truly deserve! We only serve the heartiest, healthiest dishes, lovingly prepared and generously served. Being at Akai is being with family! Akai is yours!
